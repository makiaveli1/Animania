How to Play Animania

Your guide to mastering this quiz:

  1. Select a Category: Choose between Anime or Manga to start the quiz.
  2. Answer Quickly: You have 10 seconds to answer each question. The timer resets for each new question.
  3. Click to Answer: Click on your chosen answer. The button will turn green for a correct answer and red for an incorrect one.
  4. Keep Score: Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
  5. Leaderboard: Enter your username after the quiz to save your score and see how you stack up!

Good luck, and may the best otaku win!
